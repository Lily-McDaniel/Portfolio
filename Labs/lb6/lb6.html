A hexadecical or hex is a six-digit, three-byte hexadecical number used in HTML, CSS, SVG, and other computing applications to represent colors. The bytes represent the red, green, and blue components of the color. One byte represents a number in the range 00 to FF in hexadecical notation, or 0 to 255 in decimal notation.
